Welcome! Sant Family



This website will collect and share our bowl picks this year!

The My Picks page will allow you to see and edit your picks. At the beggining, all your picks will be empty and you will just see the bowl games. You can click edit and make or change your pick.

The Group Picks page will show everyone's picks and allow you to view it in different ways. Such as, you can view it by game or by particpant.

The My Participants page my not be as useful to you as to other users. Each person, when creating an account, will have a participant created that represents them. You can change this name, that is automatically the username, to something else without changing your username for your account.
You can also create particpants, for others such as your children so that they don't need to create an account. They can then create picks on their "particpant" on your account. This can also be used for a spouse that doesn't want to create an account.

If you need anything else, concerning this site, then contact your local admin.
